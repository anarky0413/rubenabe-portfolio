2025 Artworks

In 2025, I pushed further into controlled color, gradient fills, and stronger silhouettes. I tried to use less color overall, but make each color decision feel more deliberate.

The work from this year focuses more on detail, contrast, form, and atmosphere. Even with fewer pieces, most of them moved closer to the visual direction I was trying to build.
