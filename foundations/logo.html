Foundations

Logo System

Design rationale

Simple, angular, and easy to reuse.

The logo uses a sharp geometric structure to create a distinctive identity that feels modern, clean, and portfolio ready. The mark works well at small sizes, while the lockup versions support use in navigation, headers, and branded page sections.

This system keeps the identity flexible by providing black, brown, cream, and white versions so the logo can adapt to different background conditions without losing legibility.

Preferred usage

Recommended combinations

  • Black on white for default documentation pages
  • Cream on gradient for navigation and hero branding
  • White on black for high contrast presentations
  • Brown on white for warm but softer branded moments

Consistency notes

Keep the system consistent

  • Do not stretch or distort the logo
  • Keep enough padding around each version
  • Use the lockup when the brand name should be explicit
  • Use the mark alone only where space is tight or repetition is unnecessary
